Common questions

Calendars not syncing

Make sure ChronoSync has full calendar access in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Calendars.

Restore Premium

Open ChronoSync › Profile and tap Restore Purchases to recover your one-time purchase.

Watch app not showing

Open the Watch app on iPhone, scroll to ChronoSync and tap Install, then wait for the sync to complete.

Something else?

Use the form above and we'll investigate. Include your iOS version and a description of the issue.
